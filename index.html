Enabling fast and secure cross border payments at near zero costs.

With the current payment system, it takes 3-5 business days for cross-border payments to process and with zero transparency and control for involved parties. International payments can cost between 5-7% in transaction costs. The current system puts a burden on the most vulnerable communities.

At Remarkable, we are building a payment system that is fair, transparent, and takes a fraction of a second to process global payments at near-zero costs.

Fighting corruption by bringing transparency.

Corruption costs the global economy a whopping 2.6 trillion dollars every year. That's 5% of the total global gross domestic product (GDP). According to the World Bank, corruption is a prime global crisis and is the single biggest reason behind inequality and injustice to the poor and most vulnerable.

Remarkable's is a new and transparent platform powered by blockchain technology, making it difficult for anyone to forge public records and tamper with them. Blockchain is a new technology that store data in a public ledger secured by complex cryptographic algorithms.
